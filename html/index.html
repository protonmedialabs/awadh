R & D Domains

The R & D focus of the hub consists:

Agriculture Automation & Information systems

The domain emphasises on sustainable data collection with affordable sensing infrastructure, data analysis with state of the art AI/ML methods, and distribution of the information with an adaptive human-computer interface for decision support and task automation.

Know more

IoT Systems

IoT is the backbone of most agriculture CPS systems. IoT prototypes do not automatically scale to large IoT systems. One should understand the problems associated with the system development processes before attempting to go from prototype to large scale systems. We aim to build scalable IoT solutions for management and tracking of agriculture products.

Know more

Water Treatment & Management

Water treatment and management domain deals with the water quality assessment, treatment and management using IoT enabled nanobubble based advanced oxidation, aeration, aerobic digestion and dissolved air flotation unit in wastewater and comparing its performance with the existing methods.

Know more

Water & Soil Assessment Processes

Water and Soil Quality Assessment Processes domain aims at developing and utilizing cyber physical system enabled tools to address environmental quality issues pertaining to agriculture and water domain that are of regional and national importance, including eco-friendly technologies for soil and groundwater contaminant remediation, impact assessment of natural and/or anthropogenic activities, and soil/water quality assessment.

Know more

Stubble Management Systems & Urban Farming

Stubble Management Systems and Urban Farming domain focus on the removal of stubble, post-harvest usage of waste created, urban farming and development of new machines for agriculture. The aim is to create novel and sustainable system for Indian continent with reuse of crop residues (such as bio-char development or paper making), and land resource development in urban areas (such as vertical farming, hydroponics, etc.).

Know more

Nuclear Applications in Agriculture & Water

Nuclear application in agriculture and water aim to identify the sources of soil/water contamination by radioactive pollutants, determine the distributions of naturally occurring and artificially infused radioisotopes, and to develop advanced chemical/physical methods and techniques to remove the radio-contamination from the soil and water, and food irradiation for self life enhancement of agri products.

The hub supports R&D in the domain of Agriculture & Water, and provides incubation resources and support to technology-based start-ups towards sustainable agriculture.

The R & D focus of the hub consists

  • Water/industry discharge assessment, treatment, and management
  • Advancement of land resources and farm preparation
  • CPS in Agriculture automation
  • Application of AI/ML in precision farming
  • CPS in harvesting and post-harvesting
  • The deployment of IoT systems in the domain of Agriculture & Water

The technology advancements achieved at iHub - AWaDH will be deployed in the following technology domains

  • Food Processing Industry
  • Rural Development
  • Fisheries
  • Skill Development and Entrepreneurship
  • Textiles industry for discharge management
  • Electronics and IT Industry for the development of IoT devices
  • Fertilizer industry for the optimized combinations of nutrients
  • Food and Public Distribution
  • Atomic Energy
  • NITI Aayog
  • Implementation of right farming practices in terms of preparation of farmland, pre/post-harvesting, delivery of the agricultural commodities to the consumers, preservation, and storage without affecting the environment

Some of the technologies that are being developed at iHub - AWaDH

  • Design and development of IoT enabled nano-bubble /oxygen generators for water treatment and remediation of rural domestic and industrial wastewater
  • IoT based CPS for the water quality assessment and management
  • Technology for the generation and application of crop-residue derived Biochar
  • CPS for mapping the naturally occurring and artificially infused radioisotopes in soil and water
  • AI-based CPS for optimized fertilizer / pesticide application
  • Design and development of IoT enabled Harvester-cum-Bailer machine for Stubble Management
  • Extra-ordinary and diversified applications of stubble for better environment and new livelihoods opportunities
  • Urban farming
  • Automation in agriculture towards precision farming in Rural and Urban settings
  • IoT in the Domain of AgriTech
